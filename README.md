# Midjourney Prompts

This repository documents my approach to generating images with generative AI, using Midjourney and other tools. Here you'll find my workflow, prompt engineering notes, and examples of prompts with parameters. Feel free to explore and add your own ideas.

## Making Images with AI

### 01. Select Software

Before diving into creating an artwork, I decide on the software. Not every program suits all requirements – one might excel at photo editing, another at drawing, and a third at 3D projects. I use multiple tools to find the most suitable option for my work.

### 02. Prompt Engineering

To achieve the desired result when creating an image with a neural network, it is crucial to provide it with accurate prompts. This process requires skills from various disciplines, including writing, photography, artistry, programming, and other specialties. I constantly consider composition, lighting, color, and objects to enhance the final outcome.

### 03. Examples with Prompts

Below are some example prompts illustrating my process. For each image generation I define a goal, select key elements, consider composition and lighting, specify the color palette, describe details and textures, choose a drawing style, convey an emotional note, and provide feedback after the first generation.

#### Peony Flower Art Exhibition

```text
peony flower art exhibition with transparent glass case, in the style of ethereal and dreamlike atmosphere, minimalist stage designs, aesthetic, cherry blossoms, cubo futurism, post‑modern installation artist, glitch aesthetic --c 80 --s 100 --stylize 500 --ar 35:32 --style raw --v 6.0
```

#### Historical Bomb Scene

```text
The bomb fell from the sky for 44.4 seconds before detonating. The explosion was devastating, destroying 90% of the city. Imagine a mushroom cloud rising into the sky. The blinding glare and shock wave wiped out buildings, streets and lives. The people of Hiroshima were trapped in an inferno of fire and radiation. --ar 2:3 --s 750 --v 6.0 --style raw
```
